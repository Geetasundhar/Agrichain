About AgriChain

AgriChain is a blockchain-based platform designed to empower farmers, ensure transparency, and streamline the agricultural supply chain.

Our platform integrates a direct marketplace for farmers, blockchain-based crop traceability, and automated smart insurance to protect farmers against risks.

By removing middlemen and automating insurance claims, AgriChain ensures that farmers earn more and consumers trust their food.
